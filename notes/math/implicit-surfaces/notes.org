** What is a parametric equation?
- Instead of defining y in terms of x (e.g. \[y = f(x)\]) or x in terms of y (e.g. \[x = f(y)\]) we define x and y in terms of a third variable, called a parameter:
\[x=f(t)\]     \[y=f(t)\]
